New feature:
Build a separate optional Home / Cleaning support module for ADHD-friendly micro-tasks.

Core rule:
- Do not mix home/cleaning tasks into the main business task stream by default.
- Keep this as a separate optional lane, module, category, or toggleable section.

Goal:
Help the user start tiny household or cleaning tasks without overwhelm by using micro-tasks, low-friction prompts, energy filters, and short timed resets. Microtask-based task design can make starting easier and reduce resistance for ADHD users. [web:901]

Include:
- a reusable home/cleaning micro-task bank
- categories such as kitchen, bathroom, laundry, living area, bedroom, and general reset
- energy filters: low, medium, high
- time filters: 2-minute, 5-minute, 10-minute, 15-minute
- sprint modes: 5-minute reset, 10-minute sprint, one-song cleanup
- support for prompts like:
  - give me one tiny home task
  - give me a low-energy cleaning task
  - give me a 10-minute reset
  - give me one kitchen micro-task

Use the same Quest task-card structure:
- Title
- Why this matters
- Done looks like
- Suggested next step

Example seed tasks:
Kitchen:
- Put dirty dishes in sink
- Empty dishwasher
- Wipe one counter
- Clear one hotspot

Bathroom:
- Wipe sink
- Replace hand towel
- Empty bathroom garbage

Laundry:
- Start one load
- Fold 5 items
- Put away one clothing category

Living area:
- Gather cups
- Clear coffee table
- Put 5 things away

Bedroom:
- Make bed
- Put clothes in hamper
- Clear one surface

General reset:
- Put 5 things away
- Throw out visible trash
- Carry one item back to its room
- Do a 5-minute reset
